Pork & Apples with Cider Cream Sauce

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs pork tenderloin, cut into 12 (2-inch thick) slices (medallions)
  • fresh cracked pepper
  • 2 TBLS butter
  • 2 medium shallots, thinly sliced (about ½ cup)
  • 1 ½ tsp honey
  • ½ cup apple cider
  • ½ tsp finely chopped fresh rosemary
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 1 TBLS oil
  • 3 Granny Smith apples, unpeeled & thickly sliced
  • 1 TBLS finely chopped fresh rosemary
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • ¼ cup reduced sodium chicken broth
  • ¼ cup heavy cream
  • freshly ground pepper
